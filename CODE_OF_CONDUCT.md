# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want DPI TRS Tracker to be a respectful, practical, and useful open-source space for people interested in pharmaceutical manufacturing operations, TRS/OEE analytics, GMP workflow support, and industrial software.

Participants are expected to communicate professionally and constructively.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent while still reviewing carefully.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.
- Welcome contributors from production, quality, engineering, and software backgrounds.
- Use fictional data in public examples.
- Protect confidential manufacturing and personal information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Sharing confidential production, supplier, employee, patient, or batch-sensitive data.
- Presenting unvalidated project outputs as approved GMP decisions.
- Pressuring contributors to disclose private workplace information.

## GMP and safety note

This project is decision-support software and is not validated GMP software by default. Discussions should respect quality, safety, and data-integrity boundaries.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates these expectations. Serious or repeated violations may lead to blocked participation.
